EDUCATION

Swinburne University of Technology,  PhD in astronomy 2022 - expected to graduate September 2025

I am working on the project “Understanding how circumgalactic gas drives galaxy evolution”, under the supervision of Prof. Glenn Kacprzak. My thesis explores the effects of interactions between galaxies in their circumgalactic medium.

Universidad de Chile, MSc in astronomy (2018 - 2021)

I worked on my thesis titled  “A study of the invisible circum-galactic medium around a z=0.77 star-forming galaxy using gravitational-arc tomography”, under the supervision of Prof. Sebastián López.

Universidad de Chile,  BSc in astronomy (2014 - 2017)

RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Swinburne University of Technology, Understanding how the circumgalactic medium drives galaxy evolution (2022-present)

Conducted a comprehensive analysis of the circumgalactic medium in galaxy groups using multi-wavelength spectroscopy (HST/COS, Keck/KCWI). This project is part of my aforementioned PhD thes isunder the supervision of Prof. Glenn Kacprzak.

Universidad de Chile, “Arc-tomography” of the circumgalactic medium (Second semester 2017 - 2021)

Applied gravitational-arc tomography to study the CGM of a z=0.77 star-forming galaxy, identifying orientation effects in CGM absorption features. This project is part of my previously mentioned M.Sc. thesis, under the supervision of Prof. Sebastián López

Universidad de Chile, Looking for binaries in Ophiucus (First semester 2017)

I worked looking for binary stars in the Ophiucus molecular cloud, under the supervision of postdoc Alice Zurlo.

Universidad de Chile, Summer Internship (Summer 2017).

I did a small research project under the supervision of Prof. Valentino González, about characterising the properties of high-redshift galaxies.

Universidad de Chile, Characterising the variability of blazars using spectral power density (First semester 2019)

I worked on characterising the spectral power density of blazars’ light curves using Approximate Bayesian Computation, under the supervision of Walter Max-Moerbeck.
